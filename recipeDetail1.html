Spaghetti & Meatballs

With Marinara & Parmesan Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 lb. extra-lean ground beef
  • 1/4 cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 Tbsp. milk
  • 1/3 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Shredded Parmesan Cheese, divided
  • 1/4 lb. spaghetti, uncooked, broken in half
  • 1/3 jar (24 oz.) CLASSICO Marinara with Plum Tomatoes & Olive Oil Pasta Sauce

Instructions:
  1. Heat oven to 375ºF.
  2. Mix first 5 ingredients and 1/4 cup cheese just until blended; shape into 18 meatballs. Place on rimmed baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray.
  3. Bake 15 min. or until done (160ºF). Meanwhile, cook spaghetti as directed on package, omitting salt. About 5 min. before spaghetti and meatballs are done, cook sauce in large saucepan on medium-low heat until heated through, stirring occasionally.
  4. Drain spaghetti; place on platter. Add meatballs to sauce; stir until evenly coated. Spoon over spaghetti; top with remaining cheese.
Cooking Time:

30 Min(s) 30 Min(s) Prep
