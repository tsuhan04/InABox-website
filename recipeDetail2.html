Grilled BBQ Chicken

With House Mac N Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Original Barbecue Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp. ground red pepper (cayenne)
  • 1/3 whole chickens (7 lb.)
  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Light Mayo
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 head cabbage (2 lb.), shredded
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/3 fresh jalapeño peppers, stemmed, seeded and thinly sliced
  • 1/3 pkg. (7-1/4 oz. each) KRAFT Mac & Cheese, prepared as directed on package using the Light Prep directions

Instructions:
  1. Heat greased grill to medium heat.
  2. Mix barbecue sauce and red pepper. Use kitchen shears or sharp knife to spatchcock chickens. (See tip.) Open chickens butterfly-style; pound to even thickness.
  3. Grill, cut sides down, 40 to 50 min. or until done (165ºF), turning after 20 min. and brushing with barbecue sauce mixture for the last 5 min.
  4. Mix mayo and lime juice in large bowl. Add cabbage, cilantro and jalapeño peppers; mix lightly. Serve with the chicken and Dinners.
Cooking Time:

1 Hr(s) 5 Min(s) 15 Min(s) Prep 50 Min(s) Cook
